Bedside Robotic Assistant

Proposal

The lack of efficient and real-time access to patients' medical information at their bedside in healthcare facilities is a critical issue. To address this, we propose the development of a bedside robotic assistant that utilizes QR codes to access and update patients' digital medical records.

The robotic assistant will streamline the process of retrieving essential patient data, allowing healthcare professionals to make more informed decisions, provide personalized care, and enhance overall patient outcomes.
